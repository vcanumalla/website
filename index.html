I am a second year graduate student in electrical engineering at Stanford. Previously, I graduated from the Allen School at the University of Washington (UW CSE) where I studied computer science.

My research interests are in the intersection of programming languages and computer architecture. Most recently I worked on using program synthesis in automating hardware compilation of FPGAs. At the University of Washington, I was very fortunate to be advised by Zachary Tatlock and Gus Smith.
